Korea's Travel Distribution Landscape

Korea's travel distribution ecosystem is complex and relationship-driven. The top 5 online travel agencies (OTAs) command over 70% of online bookings, while offline travel agencies — especially corporate and group travel specialists — still generate significant volume for flights, cruises, and package tours.

Building distribution in Korea requires both digital channel integration and human sales relationships. Diplat Korea has built and maintained these relationships for 12+ years. When we introduce your product to Korea's distribution network, we bring trust, credibility, and existing relationships — not cold calls.
