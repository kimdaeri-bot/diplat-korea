Expert advisory services for global travel and tourism brands entering or expanding in the Korean market. We help you make the right decisions — fast.

What We Do

The Korean travel market is one of the most dynamic and fastest-growing in Asia — but it operates by its own rules. Consumer behavior, digital infrastructure, regulatory environment, and distribution channels are all distinctly Korean. Without expert local knowledge, global brands risk misaligned positioning, wasted marketing spend, and delayed market entry.

Diplat Korea's Consulting & Strategy service gives you the competitive edge you need. We conduct deep market research, map the competitive landscape, identify the right distribution partners, and build a go-to-market plan tailored to your brand's goals and budget.

Our Approach

01

Discovery & Audit

We start by understanding your brand, product, current market position, and objectives. We then audit the Korean competitive landscape and identify whitespace opportunities.

02

Market Research

Primary and secondary research into Korean consumer behavior, travel spend patterns, seasonal demand, competitor pricing, and distribution channel effectiveness.

03

Strategy Development

We develop a comprehensive market entry or growth strategy — covering positioning, pricing, channel mix, marketing budget allocation, and KPI framework.

04

Execution Roadmap

Deliverables include a phased action plan with clear milestones, timelines, responsible parties, and success metrics — ready to implement from day one.
