We design, build, and operate travel booking platforms — OTAs, B2B portals, white-label systems — fully optimized for Korea's market, users, and payment ecosystem.

Building for the Korean Market

Launching a travel platform in Korea requires more than translating an existing system. Korean consumers expect mobile-first experiences, Kakao Pay and Naver Pay integrations, real-time chat support, and performance standards set by Korea's hyper-competitive tech ecosystem.

Diplat Korea's development team builds travel platforms from the ground up — or extends your existing global platform for the Korean market. We combine travel industry expertise with full-stack engineering capability to deliver systems that convert.

What We Build

🌐

OTA / Booking Websites

Full-featured online travel agency platforms with search, booking, payment, and post-sale management — in Korean and mobile-optimized.

📱

Mobile Apps

iOS and Android travel apps with Korean UX standards, push notifications, local payment methods, and offline functionality.
