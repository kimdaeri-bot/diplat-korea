About us

DIPLAT KOREA is an expert in the Korean travel market as a Sales & Marketing and IT/OTA company. With over 12 years of experience in airlines, OTAs, car rentals, hotels, railways, and travel technology, we have become a leading provider of sales, marketing, and platform solutions within Korea's travel ecosystem.

We focus on launching and growing global brands in the Korean market, driving measurable revenue through sales, marketing, and localization strategies. Our team offers tailor-made services in branding, marketing, localization, distribution, and consulting — customized to each client's business goals and market needs.

Additionally, we specialize in API integration and travel tech development, allowing us to build, connect, and operate OTA platforms directly for our global partners.
